# Product & Usage FAQ

Q: What types of admission tickets do you resell?
A: We resell admission tickets for domestic attractions including theme parks, museums, observation decks, and seasonal exhibitions. The full current catalog is shared in your partner portal.

Q: Are the tickets e-tickets or physical tickets?
A: All tickets are e-tickets (mobile voucher or QR code) delivered electronically — there is no physical ticket mailing for any product in the current catalog.

Q: How long is a ticket valid for after purchase?
A: Standard validity is 90 days from the purchase date unless the product page states a specific use-by or fixed event date.

Q: Can a ticket be used on any day, or is a fixed date required?
A: Most attraction tickets are open-dated and can be used on any day within the validity window. Date-specific or timed-entry products are clearly labeled as such on the product page.

Q: What happens if a traveler loses their e-ticket voucher?
A: The e-ticket can be resent from the original booking confirmation email, or your agency can retrieve and resend it from the partner portal using the booking reference.

Q: Are tickets transferable between travelers?
A: Tickets are generally transferable before redemption unless the product page states the ticket is name-registered. Once redeemed at the venue, a ticket cannot be reused or transferred.

Q: Do children or seniors get discounted admission?
A: Where the venue offers a child or senior rate, a separate discounted ticket type is listed in the catalog. Discounts cannot be applied retroactively to a standard adult ticket.

Q: What languages does the venue signage and audio guide support?
A: Language support varies by venue. Common venues offer English, Korean, Japanese, and Chinese signage or audio guides; specific language availability is listed on each product page.

Q: How do travelers redeem the e-ticket at the venue?
A: Travelers present the QR code or mobile voucher at the venue's ticket gate or a designated partner counter for scanning; no separate paper exchange is required for most venues.

Q: What is included in the standard admission ticket — are there any extra entry fees on-site?
A: The standard admission ticket covers general entry to the venue. Optional add-ons (special exhibitions, locker rental, equipment rental) are not included unless explicitly bundled in the product title.

Q: Can our agency get high-resolution images or marketing copy for a product?
A: Yes, marketing assets (images, descriptions, logos) for each product are available for partner use in the partner portal's marketing resources section.

Q: Is there a cancellation policy for tickets before redemption?
A: Most open-dated tickets can be cancelled before the validity window expires per the venue's standard policy, shown on the product page. Date-specific or already-redeemed tickets are typically non-cancellable.
